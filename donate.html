Where it goes

Every dollar, accounted for.

01

Sanjivani Hospital

Not-for-profit care for 100+ villages around Kuchipudi, Andhra Pradesh, from prenatal care to surgeries.

02

Local programs

Free and low-cost cultural workshops and camps that teach San Diego kids and bring the community together.

03

Supplies & materials

Art supplies, ingredients and materials so our youth leaders can keep showing up and giving back.

Donations are processed by SiliconAndhra, a registered nonprofit, through its official Sanjivani giving page and are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law. Prefer to give another way, or want to give in person at an event? Email us at fylhsandiego@gmail.com.
